How to play: Sort trash according to category

  • Paper Includes: Newspapers, magazines, catalogs, office paper, egg cartons, Kraft paper, phone books, junk mail, brown paper bags, feed sacks, books, and shredded paper.
  • Cardboard Includes: Basic corrugated boxes or sheets, cereal boxes, shoe boxes, or similar containers. Please remove all Styrofoam, plastic bags, and any other packaging.
  • Plastic and metal includes: milk jugs, soap bottles, butter tubs, yogurt cups, buckets, pop and water bottles Tin cans, pop cans, aluminum foil, frying pans, cookie sheets, license plates, stainless steel kitchen items, cast iron skillets, brass items, bikes, electrical cords, electric motors, and wall chargers.
  • Landfill Includes: paper coffee cups, clothing, plastic bags, Cigarette buts, Food packaging, Aluminium cans
  • Compost Includes: Fruits, vegetables, Tea bags, used facial tissues,Pencil shavings
